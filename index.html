History of Tea

When did tea first appear in the world?

Legend says that Emperor Shen Nung discovered tea in 2732 BC when leaves from a wild tree fell into his boiling water.

Tea was traded from China to Tibet, the Arabs, the Turks, and the Indian nomadic tribes in the Himalayas by the 8th century AD. It reached Europe in the 16th century and Britain in the 17th century.
